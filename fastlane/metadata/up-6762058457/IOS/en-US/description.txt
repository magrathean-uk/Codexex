Codexex is a native iOS utility for tracking Codex usage at a glance on iPhone and iPad.

It shows 5-hour and weekly usage, reset times, Peaks/Cycle/Month history views, a weekly pace marker, and forecast details so you can see whether you are on track or likely to go over the plan.

Version 3 adds adaptive light, dark, and system appearance, flexible iPhone and iPad layouts, improved history forecasting, 24-hour snooze, and a cleaner Settings experience.

Codexex supports ChatGPT sign-in and Preview Mode for App Review and offline testing. Sign-in uses Safari and a device-code flow. Quota reads stay on this device with no Mac bridge, no browser cookies, and no scraping.
