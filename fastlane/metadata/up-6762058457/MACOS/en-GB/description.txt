Codexex is a native macOS menu bar utility for tracking Codex usage at a glance.

It shows 5-hour and weekly usage, reset times, a 30-day history chart, a weekly pace marker, and forecast details so you can see whether you are on track or likely to go over the plan.

Codexex supports ChatGPT sign-in, Preview Mode for App Review and offline testing, and a compact helper-based release flow that keeps the app fast and self-contained.
