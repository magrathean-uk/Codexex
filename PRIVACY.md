# Privacy Policy for Codexex

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Codexex is a menu bar app for viewing Codex usage.

## Data we collect

Codexex does not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking data.

## Data stored on device

Codexex stores the following locally on your Mac:

- account state
- usage history
- preview mode settings
- onboarding and window preferences

## Authentication

If you use ChatGPT sign-in, Codexex uses the supported sign-in flow to request access for your account. Account state stays on your device.

## Network access

Codexex talks to OpenAI and Apple services only to support sign-in, quota lookup, and App Store review. It does not sell or share your data.

## Contact

Questions can be sent through the app support page or the project issue tracker.
